About

I’m a west coast transplant who had no idea what I wanted to study in college, except that I wanted to study everything. Now in my third year at Northeastern University in Boston, I'm majoring in journalism and information science. These fields, which do not intersect curriculum-wise but do in industry, teach me widely applicable skills while allowing me to pursue more specific interests. I'm an aspiring digital and data journalist especially interested in covering science, health, technology, and media. In my free time, I'm learning front end development through resources such as freeCodeCamp. I aim to create interactive, multimedia tools for telling stories. Check out my projects, writing, and other links below!
